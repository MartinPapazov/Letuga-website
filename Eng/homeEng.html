Welcome to guest house Letuga

Villa „ Letuga offers excellent conditions for an unforgettable holiday, weekend, meeting with friends or family celebration in a quiet place in nature.

The villa is located in the suburbs Cholakova Cheshma 3 km from Sunny Beach resort, towards the village of Kosharitsa. The area is at the foothills of the Balkan mountains.

We offer cozy and comfortable atmosphere in a forest, clean air and birdsong.

In the large garden with lush and varied vegetation may be deserved rest with a drink in hand, away from the severe lifestyle.

At your disposal is a pool with sun loungers and umbrellas, kitchen with barbecue where everyone can perform their culinary fantasies.

Provided entertainment for our younger guests can play undisturbed in the children's playground and a large fully secured courtyard also improve their athletic skills in table tennis.

And for those who need to be connected to the internet, there is Wi-Fi.

The house is open year round and during the cold days can spend a pleasant front of the fireplace in the tavern.
